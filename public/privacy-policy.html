Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 3, 2026  ·  poolremodelquote.com

Pool Remodel Quote ("we," "us," or "our") operates poolremodelquote.com. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect when you use our website, how we use it, and your choices regarding that information.

Information We Collect

When you submit the free estimate request form, we collect:

  • Your name, phone number, and email address
  • Your pool's street address, city, state, and zip code
  • Details about the work you're requesting
  • Any photos you choose to upload
  • Any notes you provide about your project

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you submit solely to:

  • Contact you to schedule a free on-site estimate
  • Share your contact and project details with the licensed contractor assigned to your inquiry
  • Send you appointment confirmations, quote details, and project updates via text message and email
  • Process your deposit payment if you choose to move forward with a job

We do not sell your information to third parties. We do not use your information for advertising purposes unrelated to your pool remodel inquiry.

Text Message Communications (SMS)

By submitting the estimate request form, you consent to receive text messages from Pool Remodel Quote at the phone number you provide. These messages may include:

  • A confirmation that your request was received
  • Appointment reminders and scheduling confirmations
  • Your estimate details
  • Job status updates (deposit received, start date confirmed, etc.)
  • A review request when your job is complete

Message frequency varies by project stage. Standard message and data rates may apply. You may opt out at any time by replying STOP to any text message. Reply HELP for assistance. Opting out will not affect your ability to receive your estimate or have your job completed.

We use Twilio to deliver SMS communications. Your phone number is shared with Twilio solely for message delivery. Twilio's privacy policy is available at twilio.com/en-us/legal/privacy.

Information Shared with Contractors

Your name, phone number, email address, pool address, project notes, and submitted photos are shared with the licensed pool remodeling contractor assigned to your inquiry. That contractor will contact you to schedule your estimate and, if you proceed, to complete your project.

Photos and Uploaded Files

Photos you upload are stored securely and shared only with your assigned contractor. They are used to assess your project scope before the on-site estimate. We do not use your photos for marketing without your express written consent.

Payments

If you choose to pay a deposit, payment is processed by Stripe through your contractor's Stripe account. We do not store your full credit card number. Stripe's privacy policy is available at stripe.com/privacy.

Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to complete your project and maintain reasonable business records. You may request deletion of your information at any time by contacting us below. We will remove your data from our systems within 30 days of a verified request, except where retention is required by law or for legitimate business purposes (such as completed transaction records).

Security

We use industry-standard technical safeguards to protect your information, including encrypted database storage and restricted access controls. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the effective date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
